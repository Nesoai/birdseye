Questions, answered

Is it really two business days?

Yes for the vast majority of parcels. When a jurisdiction’s records require a phone call or a site is unusually complex, it can take an extra day — and we tell you before you wait, not after.

Where do you cover?

All 50 states. Our model and research process are nationwide; the human review is what keeps local quirks honest.

How is AI used — can I trust it?

AI does the fast part: pulling zoning codes, GIS layers, overlays, and records in minutes instead of days. A senior land-use professional verifies every finding and writes the recommendation. Nothing ships on AI’s word alone.

Is this legal or engineering advice?

No. Reports are professional planning research and analysis — a decision-grade starting point. Final determinations always rest with the jurisdiction, and legal questions belong with your attorney.

What do I receive?

A clean PDF (typically 10–15 pages) with maps, the zoning read, the approval path, risk flags, jurisdiction contacts, and next steps. Data audits deliver corrected datasets in your format.

What if I need changes?

One revision is included with every report. If we simply can’t produce your report, you don’t pay.

Read this: Bird’s Eye reports and the free instant read are professional research and planning analysis, not legal, engineering, or survey advice, and not a guarantee of approval. Zoning and entitlement outcomes are determined solely by the applicable jurisdiction. Verify all findings with the jurisdiction and your counsel before transacting.
